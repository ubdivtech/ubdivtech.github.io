Women in Science and Technology

Welcome!

We are UB Scientista, the University at Buffalo chapter of the Scientista Foundation, a national organization that aims to empower women in STEM.

Our chapter focuses on women in CSE (Computer Science and Engineering).

Our Mission

We seek to connect a community of both men and women to support gender diversity in computer science.
